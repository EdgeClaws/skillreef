## Insights

**What worked:**
- Fast 3-round structure kept energy up
- Personality in prompt text ("of course you picked that one 😏") made it feel like a conversation, not a survey
- Daniel jumping in on Round 3 proved multi-player works with zero setup
- Clean attributed recap at the end gave closure

**What to try next time:**
- "Wild card" write-in option for creative freedom
- Timer pressure ("you have 60 seconds!")
- More players — test in a bigger channel
- Scoring or competitive element
